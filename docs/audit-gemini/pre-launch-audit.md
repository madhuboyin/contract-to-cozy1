# Pre-Launch Product Audit: ContractToCozy (CtC)
## Final Strategy: "Your Home, Handled."

**Executive Stance:** ContractToCozy is an **Essential Homeowner Service**, not a feature catalog. We are building a **Home Command Center** that removes uncertainty and protects home wealth with minimal user effort. 

The product's "Moat" is its deep technical engines. The product's "Surface" is a calm, intelligent, and outcome-driven interface that answers: **"What matters today?"**

---

### THE STRATEGIC PIVOT: THE 6 PRIMARY JOBS

We have moved from a "Toolbox" to a **Command Center** organized around these 6 Jobs-to-be-Done:

1.  **Today:** What matters right now (Urgent alerts + Successes).
2.  **My Home:** The high-level overview and health of the asset.
3.  **Protect:** Defense (Maintenance, Risks, Insurance, Warranties).
4.  **Save:** Optimization (ROI, Taxes, Energy, Refinance).
5.  **Fix:** Resolution (Issues, Quotes, Booking, Completion).
6.  **Vault:** Memory (The "Black Box" of records, photos, and manuals).
