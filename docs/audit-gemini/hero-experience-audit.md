# SECTION 3 — Hero Experience Audit: ContractToCozy (CtC)

**Auditor Note:** You cannot launch a "Home Management Platform." It is too broad, too boring, and too much work. You must launch a **solution to a specific, high-anxiety problem.** By auditing your 40+ existing routes, I have extracted the three "Wedges" that are technically ready and emotionally resonant.

---

### Hero 1: The "Zero-Surprise" Maintenance Engine
*Consolidates: `maintenance`, `checklist`, `seasonal`, `actions`, `home-events`*

*   **User Problem Solved:** The "What am I forgetting?" anxiety that leads to $10,000 repair bills.
*   **Why Users Care:** Owning a home feels like waiting for something to break. This provides a sense of control.
*   **Why It Is Urgent:** Home systems (HVAC, Roof) are failing at record rates due to extreme weather; proactive care is no longer optional.
*   **Why It Is Monetizable:** Lead-gen fees from booking pros directly from a task; "Premium" automated scheduling.
*   **Why It Is Differentiated:** Most apps are static lists; CtC uses property data + Gemini to create *contextual* tasks (e.g., "Your 2015 HVAC needs a flush before this heatwave").
*   **Why It Creates Trust:** Showing the "Reasoning" (e.g., "Manufacturer recommendation for Model X") proves the app isn't just making work.
*   **What Must Improve:** Unify the 5+ task-related routes into one "Action Feed."
*   **Suggested UI Entry:** A "Daily Snapshot" card at the top of the dashboard.
*   **Marketing Headline:** *"The end of 'I forgot to check that.' Personal maintenance, automated."*

---

### Hero 2: The AI "Home Vault" (Claims Readiness)
*Consolidates: `inventory`, `documents`, `warranties`, `insurance`*

*   **User Problem Solved:** The nightmare of "Where is that receipt?" during an insurance claim or a warranty repair.
*   **Why Users Care:** In a disaster, you can't prove what you owned. This is "Wealth Protection."
*   **Why It Is Urgent:** Insurance premiums are skyrocketing; having a documented home "Health Record" can help negotiate rates or settle claims faster.
*   **Why It Is Monetizable:** Subscription for "Unlimited Secure Storage"; Concierge document organization.
*   **Why It Is Differentiated:** "Magic" parsing. User snaps a photo -> Gemini extracts Serial, Model, Warranty Exp, and Manual Link.
*   **Why It Creates Trust:** Seeing your actual documents and extracted data instantly creates a "Digital Twin" of the home.
*   **What Must Improve:** The OCR/Extraction speed and accuracy UI (showing the user what was found).
*   **Suggested UI Entry:** A "Vault" tab with a prominent "Quick Upload" camera button.
*   **Marketing Headline:** *"Your home's black box. One photo to protect everything you own."*

---

### Hero 3: The "Coverage Intelligence" Audit
*Consolidates: `risk-radar`, `coverage-intelligence`, `risk-premium-optimizer`*

*   **User Problem Solved:** Overpaying for insurance while still having "hidden" coverage gaps.
*   **Why Users Care:** Everyone hates their insurance company but doesn't know how to fix it without a 4-hour phone call.
*   **Why It Is Urgent:** Homeowners insurance is in a national crisis; users are desperate for "Optimization" tools.
*   **Why It Is Monetizable:** Referral fees for switching to a better-matched policy; Premium "Risk Audit" reports.
*   **Why It Is Differentiated:** It connects *inventory* to *policy*. (e.g., "You have $20k in electronics but only $5k in coverage").
*   **Why It Creates Trust:** It uses the user's *actual* data to save them *actual* money.
*   **What Must Improve:** Needs a "Policy Uploader" that Gemini can read to identify gaps.
*   **Suggested UI Entry:** A "Savings & Risk" widget on the dashboard.
*   **Marketing Headline:** *"Stop overpaying for the wrong insurance. Audit your coverage in 60 seconds."*

### Executive Verdict: What should CtC be known for at launch?

CtC should **NOT** be known as a "Home Dashboard." It should be known as:

> **"The Homeowner's Black Box & Action Center."**

**The Launch Narrative:**
"You wouldn't fly a plane without a black box; why run a $500,000 asset without one? ContractToCozy uses AI to build a secure record of your home (The Vault) and tells you exactly what to do to protect its value (The Action Center)."

**Focus for the next 90 days:** 
Stop building the "Do-Nothing Simulator" or "Tax Appeal" tools. Make the **Camera -> Vault -> Action** loop so fast and impressive that a user tells their neighbor about it. That is your launch wedge.
