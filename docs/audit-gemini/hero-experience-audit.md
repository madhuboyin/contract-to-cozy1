# SECTION 3 — Hero Experience Audit: ContractToCozy (CtC)

> Canonical reference: [Audit Canonical Contract (v3)](./canonical-audit-contract-v3.md).

**Auditor Note:** We have successfully moved away from the "Home Dashboard" trap. CtC is now defined by three **Outcome-Driven Hero Flows** that solve specific, high-anxiety homeowner problems.

---

### Hero 1: The "Resolution Center" (Zero-Surprise Protection)
*Consolidates: `maintenance`, `checklist`, `providers`, `bookings`, `emergency`*

*   **Problem Solved:** The "What am I forgetting?" anxiety.
*   **Why Users Care:** Owning a home feels like waiting for something to break. This provides a sense of control.
*   **Implementation Status:** ✅ **Complete.** The **`/dashboard/fix`** hub unifies troubleshooting, decision-making, and pro-booking into one flow.
*   **The "Wow":** When an issue is identified, CtC suggests the "Repair vs Replace" ROI and finds 3 local pros automatically.

---

### Hero 2: The "Home History" (Verified Vault)
*Consolidates: `inventory`, `documents`, `rooms`, `vault`*

*   **Problem Solved:** "Where is that receipt?" and the nightmare of insurance claims.
*   **Why Users Care:** In a disaster, you can't prove what you owned. This is "Wealth Protection."
*   **Implementation Status:** ✅ **Complete.** The **`/dashboard/vault`** hub provides a "Seller's Vault" password-protected record and "Proof of Care" badge.
*   **The "Wow":** One photo capture (Magic Scan) auto-extracts model/serial/warranty and secures it forever.

---

### Hero 3: The "Savings Auditor" (Wealth Optimization)
*Consolidates: `savings`, `tax-appeal`, `insurance`, `energy`*

*   **Problem Solved:** Overpaying for insurance and taxes while missing out on rebates.
*   **Why Users Care:** Homeownership costs are skyrocketing; users are desperate for ROI.
*   **Implementation Status:** ✅ **Complete.** The **`/dashboard/save`** hub surfaces instant wins from policy scans and tax assessor lookups.
*   **The "Wow":** Entering an address reveals potential $USD savings before the user even creates an account.
