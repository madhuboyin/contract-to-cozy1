# SECTION 11 — The Great Curation: "Surface" vs "Engine"

**Auditor Note:** In a pre-launch product, "Showing Everything" is a sign of insecurity. A premium brand shows the **Value**, but hides the **Plumbing.** We are no longer "Removing" features. We are **Hiding the Engines** and **Curation the Surfaces.**

---

### The Visibility Map: "Surface" vs "Engine"

| Category | Target Surface Areas | Launch Visibility | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **🚀 Primary Surface** | Landing, Auth, Dashboard (Home), **Protect**, **Save**, **Fix**, **Vault**. | **Visible** | These define the brand. |
| **🧪 Background Engine** | `tax-appeal`, `do-nothing-simulator`, `energy`, `climate`, `savings`. | **Invisible** | Only surface when an **Outcome** is found. |
| **🛠️ Internal Only** | Admin Analytics, Knowledge Admin, Worker Jobs Monitor. | **Hidden** | Never seen by the user. |
| **🗑️ Nuke** | Gazette, Community Events (unless data exists), `home-savings` (duplicate). | **Remove** | Empty features erode trust. |

---

### Why Curation is Not Reduction

1.  **Preserves Moat:** You still have the deep code for `Tax Appeal`. You just don't force a user to click on it if they don't have an appeal due.
2.  **Focuses Attention:** A user can only do one thing at a time. The 4-Pillar Nav directs them to their **Goal.**
3.  **Surprise & Delight:** When a user's "Save" tab suddenly alerts them to a $200 energy rebate (powered by the hidden `energy` engine), it feels like magic, not a "tool."

---

### The Minimal Launch Sidebar

Your Launch Day sidebar is your **Brand Statement.**

1.  **🏠 Home** (The Daily Snap)
2.  **🛡️ Protect** (Unified maintenance & insurance)
3.  **💰 Save** (Unified ROI & tax optimization)
4.  **🚨 Fix** (Unified resolution hub)
5.  **📂 Vault** (The Digital Twin)

*The other 40+ tools are invisible until they trigger an alert or the user searches for them.*

---

### The "Drip" Engagement Strategy

*   **Week 1:** Launch the 5 Pillars.
*   **Week 2:** Trigger a `Visual Inspector` "Beta" invite to users who have an empty vault.
*   **Week 3:** Trigger the `Savings` engine to scan for insurance gaps.

**Verdict:** Your **Depth** is your moat. Your **Surfaces** are your barriers. By curating the UI to only show the "4 Jobs," you move from a "Catalog of 40 Tools" to a **"Unified Home Copilot."**
