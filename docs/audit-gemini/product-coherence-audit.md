# SECTION 5 — Product Coherence: The "Home Command Center"

**Auditor Note:** ContractToCozy currently suffers from "Feature Soup" because the **Engines** (the deep code) are being treated as **Surfaces** (the UI). We must implement a strict **Surface-to-Engine** hierarchy to transform the app from a "Tool Catalog" into a **Unified Command Center.**

---

### The Solution: Curation via 6 PRIMARY JOBS

We are no longer "Removing" functionality. We are **Curating it.**

#### 1. "Today" (Command Surface)
*   **Purpose:** Instant snapshot. "What matters now?"
*   **Engines Embedded:** Urgent alerts, daily successes, current maintenance.

#### 2. "My Home" (Asset Surface)
*   **Purpose:** Health overview. "How is my asset doing?"
*   **Engines Embedded:** Equity tracking, overall score, health summary.

#### 3. "Protect" (Defensive Jobs)
*   **Purpose:** Risk & Record Management. "Keep my home safe."
*   **Engines Embedded:** Maintenance, Insurance Gaps, Risk Radar, Warranties.

#### 4. "Save" (Optimization Jobs)
*   **Purpose:** Financial ROI. "Find me money."
*   **Engines Embedded:** Tax Appeal, Energy Audit, Savings, ROI.

#### 5. "Fix" (Resolution Jobs)
*   **Purpose:** Problem Solving. "Something's broken. Fix it."
*   **Engines Embedded:** Repair engine, Pro booking, Emergency help.

#### 6. "Vault" (Memory Layer)
*   **Purpose:** Black Box Record. "The Trusted Source of Truth."
*   **Engines Embedded:** Inventory, Documents, Photos, Rooms.

---

### Ideal Launch Information Architecture (IA)

**Primary Navigation (The 6 Pillars)**
1.  **🏠 Today**
2.  **🏡 My Home**
3.  **🛡️ Protect**
4.  **💰 Save**
5.  **🚨 Fix**
6.  **📂 Vault**

**Secondary Logic**
The 40+ engines are **invisible** until they have an **Outcome** (Win) or an **Alert** (Risk) to report. No engine exists as a standalone menu item.

---

### Final Verdict: Is CtC One Product or Many Features?

**Verdict:** **Currently Many Features, but a Unified Command Center is Ready.**

By moving from a "Tool Catalog" to a **Job-based Navigation**, you preserve your technical moat while achieving the simplicity of a world-class service. **Your Home, Handled.**
